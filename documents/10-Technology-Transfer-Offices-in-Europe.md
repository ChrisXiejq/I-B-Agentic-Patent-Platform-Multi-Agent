# Technology Transfer Offices in Europe

## Overview

This article describes the role and ecosystem of Technology Transfer Offices (TTOs) in European patent commercialisation.

---

#### What is a Technology Transfer Office (TTO)?

A TTO is a unit within a university, research organisation, or company that manages intellectual property and supports its transfer to the market. TTO activities typically include: IP strategy and portfolio management, patent filing and prosecution, licensing and assignment, spin-off creation, industry liaison, and training. TTOs connect researchers with industry and investors, helping turn inventions into products, services, and economic impact.

#### What is ASTP and how does it support European TTOs?

ASTP (Association of Science and Technology Transfer Professionals) is Europe’s main association for knowledge transfer, with over 800 members in 41 countries. It provides training (including RTTP accreditation), tools, and networking to help professionals move research into the market. ASTP is the European counterpart to AUTM (US) and is a member of the global Alliance of Technology Transfer Professionals (ATTP). It supports best practice and professional development for TTO staff.

#### What is the European TTO Circle?

The European TTO Circle brings together 35 major Research and Technology Organisations in Europe, representing about 200,000 researchers and support staff, 35,000 patents, and 4,000 startups. It serves as a forum for sharing best practices and collaboration on technology transfer and IP management. The TTO Circle supports the development of a stronger European innovation ecosystem and cross-border commercialisation.

#### What are the key competencies of an effective TTO?

Effective TTOs combine: **IP expertise** – patent law, valuation, strategy; **business development** – market analysis, deal structuring, negotiation; **networking** – links with industry, investors, and other TTOs; **project management** – pipeline management, milestone tracking; **communication** – presenting technologies to non-experts. Training programmes such as RTTP (Registered Technology Transfer Professional) and ASTP courses help build these competencies.

**References:**
- [ASTP – Training and development](https://astp4kt.eu/)
- [ATTP – Global standards](https://attp.global/)

#### How do TTOs support patent commercialisation in universities?

TTOs support commercialisation by: evaluating invention disclosures and filing patents where appropriate; developing commercialisation strategies (licensing vs spin-off); marketing technologies and identifying licensees; negotiating and drafting agreements; managing portfolios and renewal decisions; supporting spin-off creation and equity arrangements; and providing feedback to researchers. Strong TTOs are associated with higher licensing and spin-off rates in European universities.
