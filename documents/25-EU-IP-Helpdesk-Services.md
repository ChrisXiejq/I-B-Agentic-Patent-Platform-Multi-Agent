# EU IP Helpdesk Services

## Overview

This article describes the services offered by the European Commission’s IP Helpdesk to support patent and IP commercialisation.

---

#### What is the EU IP Helpdesk?

The EU IP Helpdesk is a European Commission service that provides information, training, and support on intellectual property to beneficiaries of EU-funded projects and SMEs. It offers: **helpline** – answers to IP questions; **publications** – guides, fact sheets, and articles; **training** – workshops and webinars; **Horizon IP Scan** – tailored IP support for SMEs in Horizon projects. The service is free and supports innovation and commercialisation across Europe.

**References:**
- [EU IP Helpdesk – Main website](https://intellectual-property-helpdesk.ec.europa.eu/)
- [European Commission – IP Helpdesk](https://intellectual-property-helpdesk.ec.europa.eu/about-us_en)

---

#### What is the Horizon IP Scan and who can use it?

The Horizon IP Scan is a free, tailored service for European SMEs participating in Horizon Europe (and previously Horizon 2020) projects. Local IP experts provide advice on: IP strategy, protection options, exploitation pathways, and licensing. The scan helps SMEs maximise the value of project results and navigate IP issues. It is delivered through a network of experts across Europe. SMEs can apply via the IP Helpdesk.

**References:**
- [EU IP Helpdesk – Horizon IP Scan](https://intellectual-property-helpdesk.ec.europa.eu/services/horizon-ip-scan_en)

#### What IP topics does the EU IP Helpdesk cover?

Topics include: IP basics (patents, trademarks, designs, copyright); IP in EU-funded projects (Horizon Europe); ownership and joint ownership; exploitation (licensing, transfer, assignment); IP valuation; confidentiality and trade secrets; IP in collaboration agreements; geographical indications; and IP in specific sectors. The Helpdesk does not provide legal advice but offers general information and signposting to specialised services.

**References:**
- [EU IP Helpdesk – Publications](https://intellectual-property-helpdesk.ec.europa.eu/publications_en)

#### How does the IP Helpdesk support patent commercialisation?

The Helpdesk supports commercialisation through: guides on licensing, transfer, and exploitation; information on IP valuation; training on IP management and exploitation; the Horizon IP Scan for project-based support; and the helpline for specific questions. It also highlights the Horizon Results Platform and exploitation obligations. The Helpdesk complements EPO, national offices, TTOs, and legal advisors.

#### What other EU services support IP and commercialisation?

Related services include: **Horizon Results Platform** – matchmaking for project results; **EIC (European Innovation Council)** – funding and support for innovators; **Enterprise Europe Network** – business support and partnerships; **European Patent Office** – patent granting, information, and studies; **EUIPO** – trademarks and designs. National innovation agencies and TTOs provide additional support. The IP Helpdesk can direct users to relevant resources.
